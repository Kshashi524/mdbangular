concatAll will collect all Observables and subscribe to next one when previous completes.

In the below example, there are 3 Observables but only one Observable will run at a time.

Clicking on will finish the respective inner Observable

Emits every 2 seconds Stream 1 completed
Emits every 4 seconds Stream 2 completed
Emits every 6 seconds Stream 3 completed

Concat all above streams {{concatAll}}
